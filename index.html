Why Art of Tahoe Exists

Straight from
the studio.
Into your life.

There is a particular quality of silence in the Sierra Nevada — the kind that settles in after a snowfall, or just before the light changes over the lake. Tahoe's artists have spent careers learning to hold that silence still long enough to paint it.

For too long, that work has lived behind gallery commissions, auction estimates, and warehouse storage. Art of Tahoe is built on a different premise: that the people who make extraordinary things in extraordinary places deserve to connect directly with the people who will love those things most.

We're launching soon — a curated marketplace for original Lake Tahoe art, where every piece ships from the studio that made it, every price respects the maker, and every collector knows the story behind what hangs on their wall.

01 — Know the Maker

Artist-First Curation

Every artist on Art of Tahoe is a local creator inspired to share what they love of where we live. When you collect a piece, you receive the story of its making: the trail, the morning, the particular October light that started it.

02 — Before Anyone Else

Studio-Fresh Access

Our founding members see work as it leaves the studio — sometimes before it's even named. First access to limited editions and one-of-a-kind originals that will never appear on a secondary market.

03 — Fair to the Maker

Artists Keep What They Earn

Traditional galleries take 40–60% of every sale. Here, artists retain more of the value of their work than anywhere else — which means the price you pay goes to the person who deserved it all along.

04 — A Living Collection

Beyond the Purchase

Collecting here doesn't end at checkout. Studio dispatches, seasonal field notes, and sharing your art placed in your home keep you connected to the place — and the people — behind what you own.

"Every serious collector eventually asks the same question — not what does this cost, but where did this come from and who gave their life to making it."
